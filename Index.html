About the school

The Lime Head International School was founded on a simple conviction: children who are given a global outlook, taught in small, attentive classrooms, grow into confident, capable adults anywhere in the world.

Our English-medium curriculum is benchmarked against international standards, giving students a smooth pathway to universities abroad while staying grounded in strong local values and community.

Every student is known by name, every teacher is invested in their journey, and every classroom is a place where global citizenship is practiced daily — not just taught.
